About Our Cake Shop

Zavcake was created from our love for the world of cooking and baking. We are a group of friends who share the same interest in cooking and want to share our work with others. We started this business with very little capital, selling only the cakes we made in our house to friends and family.

Our vision is to become a trusted online cake shop in Indonesia that provides high quality cakes at affordable prices. We want to be the first choice for people who want to buy cakes for their special occasions, such as birthdays, weddings, or just want to enjoy delicious cakes.

Our mission is to always provide the best service to our customers by providing a wide selection of cakes according to customer needs and tastes. We also always want to improve the quality of our cakes by continuing to learn and seek inspiration from various sources.

You have to buy cakes at our shop because we provide cakes made from high quality ingredients and processed in a hygienic way. We always prioritize customer satisfaction and always try to provide the best for every order that comes in.

In addition, our pastries have advantages in terms of taste and appearance. We always strive to create cakes that are not only delicious, but also beautiful to look at. We use fresh and high-quality ingredients to produce cakes that have a rich taste and soft texture.

The process of making cakes at our shop starts with choosing fresh and high-quality ingredients. Then, we mix these ingredients according to the recipe we made before and prepare the cake dough to be processed. After that, we turn on the oven and put the cake dough in it according to the temperature required for each type of cake. During the baking process, we always monitor the cake baking process so that the cake is perfectly cooked. After the cake is cooked, we take it out of the oven and give the finishing touch according to what the customer wants. The finished cakes are then packed neatly and ready to be sent to the customers who have ordered.

We always try to provide the best for our customers, from selecting quality ingredients to hygienic manufacturing processes. We believe that with a structured and consistent way of working, we can continue to provide delicious and quality cakes for our customers. So, don't hesitate to buy cakes at our online cake shop and taste the delicacy of our cakes for yourself.
